Lasagna

Description

This is a lasagna. It is either amazing, fragant and garlic-infused, or chewy mush.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato
  • Gasoline
  • Vehicle
  • Willingness to give up and buy a frozen one

Steps

  1. Look balefully at pasta sheets and oven
  2. Watch the Goodfellas episode of Binging with Babish
  3. Watch ten more episodes and lose track of time
  4. Go to store in your vehicle
  5. Buy frozen lasagna
  6. Prepare according to box instructions
  7. Beautiful work, you did it
